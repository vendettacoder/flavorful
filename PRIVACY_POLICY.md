# Flavorful — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-06-29_

Flavorful ("the app", "we", "us") is a recipe keeper: you paste a link to a
food-blog page and we extract the recipe so you can save, browse, and cook from
it. This policy explains what we collect, why, who we share it with, and how you
can delete it.

## Information we collect

- **Account information.** When you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive
  your email address and, where available, your name. This identifies your
  account and associates it with the recipes you save.
- **Content you create.** The recipe page URLs you submit and the recipe data
  extracted from them (title, ingredients, steps, and related metadata), plus
  whether you favorited a recipe.
- **Technical data.** Standard request information (such as IP address) used
  transiently for rate limiting and abuse prevention.

We do **not** collect precise location, contacts, photos, or device
identifiers, and we do not use third-party advertising or tracking SDKs.

## How we use your information

- To authenticate you and keep you signed in.
- To extract, store, and display the recipes you save.
- To enforce per-user limits and prevent abuse.
- To respond to support requests.

## Third parties we share data with

We share only what is necessary for these providers to operate the service:

- **Supabase** — authentication and database hosting (stores your account and
  saved recipes).
- **Google / Apple** — only when you choose that sign-in method, to verify your
  identity.
- **OpenRouter (and the AI model it routes to)** — when you submit a recipe
  URL, the text content of that public web page is sent to an AI model to
  extract the structured recipe. We do not send your account identity or other
  personal data to the AI provider; only the fetched page content is processed.

We do not sell your personal data.

## Data retention and deletion

- Your account and saved recipes are retained until you delete them.
- **You can delete your entire account and all associated data at any time
  inside the app:** open your profile (avatar in the Library) and tap
  **Delete account**. This permanently removes your saved recipes and your
  account record. The action cannot be undone.
- Some transient technical logs may be retained briefly for security, then
  discarded.

## Children

Flavorful is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age in your
region), and we do not knowingly collect their data.

## Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last
updated" date above.
